Our Water

Our water has these qualities because each drop comes from one of our seven natural mountain springs located on our farm, Hartebeeskraal nestled against the Klein Drakenstein Range near Paarl. The mountain springs are free from pollution, protected by a fortress of sandstone which acts as a natural filter, resulting in natural, clean water; so pure that our total dissolved salt content is extremely low (under 30mg/L).

  Our bottling plant is custom built and located on-site. This allows us to deliver natural water of the highest quality, direct from the mountain spring to you. Our water is strictly monitored and subjected daily to a full microbiological testing process to ensure it remains natural, pure and clean at all times.

Our team of dedicated, hard-working women have been striving since our beginnings in 1994 to provide you with convenient and easy access to our planet’s most precious resource in the form of bottled natural spring water.





OUR ENVIRONMENTAL COMMITMENT

It’s simple. We respect and value our environment. Every day the mountain springs on our farm provide us with fresh water. Every day we renew our commitment to protect and preserve the source of this provision, our planet earth, by practicing the principles of reduce, re-use and recycle. 

Reduce. We constantly strive to reduce our impact on the environment. One of the ways we achieve this is by using only lightweight plastic bottles. We also regularly evaluate the energy efficiency of our production plant in an effort to reduce our total energy consumption; for instance, we have changed the lighting throughout our facility to LED. Bottled water is known to be the best packaged beverage choice in terms of having the lightest environmental footprint. 

Re-use. We return all the outer packaging of our incoming dry stock to our suppliers for re-use.

Recycle. We only use packaging that is fully recyclable. This includes our bottles (lightweight plastic and glass), their lids (aluminium and HDPE plastic), the cardboard cartons and trays that hold our bottles, the outer plastic stretch film around the cartons and trays; even the divider boards used between our glass bottles are fully recyclable. We have added the “Please Recycle” logo to all of our products in an effort to encourage our consumers to assist us with the recycling process. In addition, we recycle any materials left over from the packaging process at our plant in Paarl. Utmost effort goes into minimising the waste left over from the packaging process, and the little that there is, we separate into glass, paper, metal, PET plastic, HDPE plastic and stretch film plastic for recycling.

  We are also proud to support PETCO, who focuses on the promotion and improvement of the waste management and recycling of post-consumer PET products. www.petco.co.za. We pay a voluntary levy to PETCO via our suppliers of PET plastic bottles. We are serious about protecting and preserving our environment. It is a priority for us.

OUR SOCIAL COMMITMENTS

At Aquabella we recognise that our success is due to our dedicated, hard-working employees. Yes, we have a state-of- the-art water bottling plant, and a unique location right next to the mountain springs that supply our water, but without our dependable, trustworthy team we would not be the accomplished water bottling company that we are today. We value the fact that each member of our team is an individual with their own strengths.

We encourage our staff to maximise these strengths and support their efforts to improve their skills by sponsoring numerous training courses ranging from Forklift Driving to Computer Skills to First Aid. We are passionate about empowering the next generation through education and support all the children of our employees from matric onwards.

The majority of our staff comes from the local communities in our valley. It is for this reason that the focus of our external social commitments is the Franschhoek, Paarl, Wellington Valley. We support a number of local schools, churches and charities in this area who align with our vision to uplift and empower previously disadvantaged people, especially woman and children.
